Use WorkItem scheduling views to choose or verify focus, not to simulate scheduler state manually. `queued`/`runnable` are ordinary resume candidates; `yielded` means parked by a continuation frame and should not be treated as blocked; `blocked`/`waiting_for_operator` mean the WorkItem owns a wait or blocker. Ending a response only yields the turn; use WaitFor, PickWorkItem, UpdateWorkItem, or CompleteWorkItem when durable scheduling state must change.
